Mint Desert

FILLING
Heat to soften 18 marshmellows & 1/2 cup milk.
While hot add green food coloring. 
Cool and add a few drops of green food coloring.  
Cool and add few drops of peppermint flavoring.  
When it thickens add 1/2 pint whippped cream (cool whip).

I DOUBLE THIS FOR 9 x 12 PAN. I don't double the crumb crust.

CRUMB CRUST
Mash 1 Lb chock. wafer crumbs (I use Oreos).
Add a few TBSP melted butter.  
Put 1/2in in bottom of pan.
Pour in mixture and cover with rest of crumbs.
